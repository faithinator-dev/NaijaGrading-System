Understanding Nigerian Grading Systems

A comprehensive guide to academic excellence across different educational levels

Secondary School Excellence

The WAEC and NECO grading systems range from A1 (75-100) to F9 (0-39). Aim for grades A1-C6 to qualify for university admission through JAMB.

University Standards

Nigerian universities use a 5.0 CGPA scale. First Class (4.50-5.00) represents exceptional performance, while 2:1 (3.50-4.49) shows strong academic achievement.

Polytechnic/Monotechnic

Polytechnics operate on a 5.0 CGPA scale focused on technical excellence. Distinction (4.50-5.00) and Upper Credit (3.50-4.49) open doors to advanced careers.
